Cappuccino Recipe

Description

A cappuccino is an Espresso based milk coffee drink. This recipe calls for a Double espresso but single or even triple shot versions may be functionally scalable albeit not recommended.

A double cappuccino uses a ratio of 1:3 shot/measures/Ounces for espresso to milk.

Ingredients

  • Light Roast Coffee beans: 14g
  • Milk: 180ml
  • Water

Steps

  1. Grind Beans to an appropriate espresso ground size
  2. Boil water, add 60g boiling water to beans in whichever brewing method used
  3. Heat milk, to 100 deg C, by steaming or frothing
  4. pour over espresso, creating a layer of foam on top of white coffee base
